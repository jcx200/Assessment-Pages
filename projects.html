Arduino

As part of my group project using the Arduino boards, we are making a Juke Box using the buzzer.

We have each selected a song to play. To play the songs we have to look through sheet music and use the tone() function to play that note based on a variable that stores a frequency to be played.

Songs being used:

  • Song By / From:
  • Psycho Muse
  • Do I Wanna Know? Artic Monkeys
